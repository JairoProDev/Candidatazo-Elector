## 12. RETENTION & ENGAGEMENT - Mantener Usuarios Activos

### 12.1 La Curva de Retención - Understanding the Math

**Por Qué Importa la Retención:**

```
ESCENARIO A: Sin Retención
├── Mes 1: 10,000 usuarios nuevos
├── Mes 2: +10,000 nuevos, -9,000 churn = 11,000 total
├── Mes 3: +10,000 nuevos, -9,900 churn = 11,100 total
└── Growth estancado, dinero quemado

ESCENARIO B: Con Retención
├── Mes 1: 10,000 usuarios nuevos
├── Mes 2: +10,000 nuevos, -3,000 churn = 17,000 total
├── Mes 3: +10,000 nuevos, -5,100 churn = 21,900 total
└── Growth compuesto, unit economics positivo

Impact:
├── A: 11,100 usuarios ($145K gastado)
├── B: 21,900 usuarios (mismo gasto)
└── Diferencia: 97% más usuarios con buena retención
```


### 12.5 Notification Strategy - The Engagement Engine

**Types of Notifications:**

```
TIER 1: CRITICAL (Always send)
├── Reply to your content
├── Friend joined platform
├── Major candidate you follow announcement
└── Live event starting (debate, etc.)

TIER 2: HIGH-VALUE (Send based on preferences)
├── Debate trending in your topic
├── Candidate match improvement
├── Weekly digest
└── Achievement unlocked

TIER 3: PROMOTIONAL (Limit frequency)
├── Feature announcement
├── Referral incentive
├── App update
└── General engagement ("Check out...")

────────────────────────────────

SMART THROTTLING

Rules:
├── Max 5 notifications/day (active users)
├── Max 3 notifications/day (casual users)
├── Max 1 notification/day (at-risk users)
├── Min 2 hours between notifications
└── Quiet hours: 11pm-7am

Prioritization:
├── If user has 10 potential notifs:
│   1. Score each by relevance
│   2. User engagement history
│   3. Send top 3-5 only
│   4. Batch rest into digest
└── Quality > Quantity

Personalization:
├── Timing: When user is active
│   • Learn from usage patterns
│   • Send when likely to engage
├── Content: What they care about
│   • Topics they follow
│   • Candidates they matched
└── Frequency: Respect their pace
    • Some want daily, some weekly
    • Let them control
```
